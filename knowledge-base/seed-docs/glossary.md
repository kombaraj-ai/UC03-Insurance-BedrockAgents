# AutoClaim Insurance Co. — Glossary & Rental Reimbursement Terms

Document ID: DOC-GLOSSARY-2026-V1

## Rental Reimbursement Addendum

Rental Reimbursement is an optional add-on. It applies only if the
policyholder's vehicle is undrivable or in a repair shop due to a covered
Comprehensive or Collision claim — it does not apply for routine
maintenance.

**Coverage limits:** Up to **$40 per day**, for a maximum of **30
consecutive days per claim** (total maximum payout: $1,200).

**Preferred vendors:** To use direct billing (policyholder pays nothing out
of pocket), rentals must be booked through the designated preferred rental
partner. If a non-preferred vendor is used, the policyholder pays upfront
and submits receipts for reimbursement within 14 days of returning the
rental.

## Glossary of Terms

- **ACV (Actual Cash Value):** The value of the vehicle at the time of loss,
  accounting for depreciation — not the original purchase price.
- **Total Loss:** A vehicle is declared a total loss when repair costs
  exceed 75% of its ACV.
- **Deductible:** The amount the policyholder pays out of pocket before
  coverage applies to the remainder of a covered loss.
- **UMPD (Uninsured Motorist Property Damage):** A separate coverage add-on
  that pays for vehicle damage caused by an uninsured or hit-and-run driver
  — not covered under standard Comprehensive or Collision.
- **Adjuster:** The AutoClaim Co. representative assigned to investigate and
  process a specific claim.
- **FNOL (First Notice of Loss):** The initial report of an incident that
  starts the claims process.
